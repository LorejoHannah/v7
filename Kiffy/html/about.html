What is Kiffy Underwear?

"Kiffy" presents a promising solution to the recurrent challenges women face during menstruation, combining the convenience of underwear with the reliability of machine-washable pads. Inspired by traditional Filipino practices and modern innovations, "Kiffy" aims to meet the needs of women by offering comfortable, high-quality, and leak-proof menstrual products. By integrating absorbent fabric pads into underwear, "Kiffy" not only addresses environmental concerns but also ensures accessibility and dependability, providing a practical alternative for women seeking sustainable and reliable period care solutions.

Made out of Cotton

Natural cotton in panty underwear and sanitary pads is breathable and soft, reducing irritation and promoting overall comfort. For pads, it also allows for better airflow which can help prevent bacterial growth. These benefits can be especially important for sensitive skin.

Made out of polyester

Polyester in panty underwear and sanitary pads is beneficial because it wicks moisture away from the body, keeping you dry and comfortable. This helps prevent leaks and irritation, especially important for panty liners and period underwear.

Made out of Bamboo Fiber

Bamboo fiber can be a comfy and eco-friendly choice for panty underwear and sanitary pads. It's naturally breathable, which helps prevent irritation and discomfort. Plus, bamboo is a renewable resource that requires less water and pesticides to grow than traditional materials.
